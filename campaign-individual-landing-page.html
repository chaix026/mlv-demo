Campaign Statement

Hello everyone! My name is Emily and I am so excited for the upcoming school year.

I started this venture because I wanted to learn how money works. Instead of just asking my parents for supplies, I want to earn them by sharing my creativity with you! I've been practicing making holiday cards and braiding bracelets all summer.

Every dollar I earn goes directly into my savings jar for my backpack, notebooks, and cool pens. Thank you for cheering me on and being part of my first business venture!
